Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder with ADHD and a TBI survivor who was suffering from chronic migraines. Struggling to maintain healthy exercise habits, he turned to what he knew worked: the distraction of video games.

By merging video games and exercise, he found a way to improve his health and happiness. GamerXise was born out of blood, sweat, tears, and a lot of trial and error, and is now being packaged to share with and benefit the whole world.
